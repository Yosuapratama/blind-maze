Instruction

Blind Maze is a game that relies on the power of memory, in the first 10 seconds you will be given time to remember the path that can be passed without being blocked by a white wall, after that the wall will disappear so you have to rely on the power of your mind, the hint button is only available 1x, this game was inspired by the 2024 student competency competition.
  1. Input Your Name & Click Start Button
  2. Use the memorizing time to memorize the way that finish
  3. Move With Arrow Up, Left, Down, Right
  4. Move your character to the finish square without hitting wall
  5. Hitting the wall or runnning out of time will decrease the number of lives
  6. And Get The Highest if you dont have live left, game over
